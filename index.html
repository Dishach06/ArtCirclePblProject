Firodia karandak

A state level intercollegiate muti-talent competition Starting back in 1974, as the brainchild of Suryakant Kulkarni, the main aim of FIRODIA KARANDAK has been to give a platform to college students for them to showcase their artistic musings for the last 50 years.

Vinodattam karandak

Vinodottam Karandak is the only competition dedicated to only comedy plays and has gained lot of popularity among theatre activists.

Dajikaka gadgil karandak

Dajikaka Gadgil Karandak is an open-to-all one act play drama competition hosted by ‘PNG Jewellers’ organised in more than 6 cities in Maharashtra and Goa.

Bharat karandak

‘Bharat Karandak’ is an inter-collegiate one-act drama competition organised by ‘Bharat Natya Sanshodhan Mandir’ .Bharat Natya Mandir, 125 years old organization , is a witness to the rich cultural heritage of Indian Theatre.

Impressions

Impressions is the national-level, annual cultural festival of CoEP Tech.This fest saw its inception in 2016, started with the motto – ‘By the artists, for the artists.

Vedant

BJ Medical College organizes Vedant as a platform for young talent to portray their dancing. It is a melting pot for different cultures, ideas, thoughts and skills. We have a melange of different cultural, sports and academic events.
